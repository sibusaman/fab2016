3D Scanning and Printing

Tasks for this module involves deep understanding about 3D printer, testing the 3D printer to it's limits to find-out its capabilities and limits. Assignments also involves exploring various 3D scanning methods like Modella MDX-20 (R.A.P.S), Kinect other hardwares like LEAP. We have to design and print an object and explain why it can't be made subtractevely, and optionally make a 3D scanner ourself, print the scanned object.

3D Printing

This is an opportunity for learning and experimenting on the 3D printer. Me and Yadu have assembled a 3d Printer (RepRap Prusa i3 ). This is a perfect opportunity to document our experience with this machine, we learned a lot while assembling and tuning the 3D printer. Documenting our experience will benefit other people who might face same troubles and will also help us because we can refer them later; our memories fade.
